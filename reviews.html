Understand the Hype!

We stopped here for brunch on a road trip to see family. So glad we did - food was delicious! We arrived around 10AM on a weekday and had a 10-15 minute wait. We did notice there were a few open tables, but we also noticed that the wait staff, though busy, did not appear stressed or overworked. I'd rather wait a bit than have them cramming as many people in as possible and overworking their staff! We also appreciated the use of mobile credit card machines. Highly recommend.
